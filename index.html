About Me

I currently a research assistant supervised by Prof. Chu-Song Chen at IVCLab, Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica. Previously, I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Information Engineering from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (NTUST), advised by Prof. Kai-Lung Hua. I also was a research development engineering Intern at ATEN INTERNATIONAL CO., LTD. in my last summer. After I graduated, I joined NTUST Center of Computer Vision and Medical Imaging as a master student and part-time research assistant, supervised by Prof. Ching-Wei Wang. Afterward, I successfully received my M.S. in Biomedical Engineering from NTUST.
